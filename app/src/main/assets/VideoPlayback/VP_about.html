The CuriousMe app has been developed as Midterm project in the second level postgraduate master in Visual Computing and Multimedia Technologies, at University of Modena and Reggio Emilia.

Key Functionality

  • Play video on targets for devices that support (devices that don't support will play full screen)
  • Optionally play video full screen in native player

Targets

Targets are stored on the Vuforia cloud web service and contain video url as a metadata package. You can insert a new pairing in the database through the proper web interface

Instructions

  • Point camera at target to view
  • Tap video to play
  • Double tap to access options menu
